About

mt80s.com plays videos from third-party sources like youtube and metacafe in a preprogrammed sequence. The purpose is to emulate people's perception of the programming of MTV from the 80s so that they feel like they are watching the station in its beloved, but unfortunately abandoned, format.

Note

mt80s.com is not affiliated with MTV, Inc. If you feel that mt80s.com is infringing on any rights you hold, please leave a comment in the comments section

Suggest

Some guidelines to be thematically consistent are:

  • Every Song Must Have a Video from the era of the song!. TOTP (Top of The Pops, a British 80s Show) is discouraged.
  • The ideal years are between 1980 and 1986, but much earlier songs like David Bowies' Space Oddity (1969) are great.
  • The priority of genres,
    1. New-wave, Alternative Rock, and General Pop
    2. Metal and Punk
    3. Electro, Pre-Gangsta Rap, and Hip-Hop
    4. Non-English Language equivalencies of the stuff above
    5. Interviews with musicians

As you type in the artist and song, there will be a result below that will show you whether it's been added already or not.
