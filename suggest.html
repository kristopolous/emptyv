About

mt80s.com streams videos from third-party US based websites in a preprogrammed sequence. The content is intended to be thematically similar to people's perception of MTV's programming from the 1980s.

Note

mt80s.com is not affiliated with MTV, Inc. If you feel that mt80s.com is infringing on any rights you hold, please leave a comment in the comments section. mt80s.com is an open source project, go there for licensing details of the code.

Suggest

Some guidelines to be thematically consistent are:

  • Every Song Must Have a Video from the era of the song!
  • The ideal years are between 1969 and 1989.
  • Interviews and non-english language songs are also welcome.

As you type in the artist and song, there will be a result below that will show you whether it's been added already or not.
