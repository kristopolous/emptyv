About

mt80s.com streams videos from third-party US based websites in a preprogrammed sequence. The content is intended to be thematically similar to people's perception of MTV's programming from the 1980s.

Note

mt80s.com is not affiliated with MTV, Inc. If you feel that mt80s.com is infringing on any rights you hold, please use the contact form. mt80s.com is an open source project, go there for licensing details of the code.
