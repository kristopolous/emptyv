Suggest A Song

Here's some general guidelines to be thematically consistent with the vision of the project:

  • Every Song Must Have a Video from the era of the song!. So Morrissey doing "Hand In Glove" in 2009 is awesome, really; but it doesn't belong here. Additionally, TOTP (top of the pops, a british 80s show), is discouraged; it's not mt80s enough.
  • The ideal years are between 1980 and 1985, but much earlier songs like David Bowies' Space Oddity (1969) are acceptable; 1989 is pushing it, unless it is stylistically old and "thought" to be from the 80s. For instance, Depeche Mode's Personal Jesus and The B-52's Love Shack both "belong" in the 80s although they are both from 1989.
  • The priority of genres are as follows:
    1. New-wave or Alternative Rock
    2. General Pop (think Go Gos)
    3. Metal or Punk
    4. Electro
    5. Pre-Gangsta Rap and Hip-Hop (NWA is thematically inconsistent)
    6. Italo Disco or EuroPop
    7. Interviews with musicians (Such as the drummer from U2)

As you type in the artist and song, there will be a result below that will show you whether it's been added already or not.
